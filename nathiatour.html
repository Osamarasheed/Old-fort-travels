Nathia Gali Day Trip

OFT would like to invite it's cherished members to join us on our weekend adventure trip, where we take you to the beautiful, enchanting Nathia Gali, where you will be able to witness, the sunshine, rain, fog & the low lying clouds all in the same trip. This trip will give you a chance to capture nature at It's best at the mesmering PIPELINE TRACK (from Ayubia to Dunga Gali) , the beautiful GOVERNOR HOUSE & the PARADISE POINT & not to mention the beautiful GREEN SPOT situated in the heart of PAF Air Base Kalabagh.

-We will depart from Islamabad at 10AM, we will track the pipeline from Ayubia to Dunga Gali. have refreshments, -we will then visit the vicinity of the governer house and paradise point in the heart of Nathia Gali -To top it all off, we will be having our final stop at KalaBagh Air base AT 5:30PM, where you will be able to view the stunning Green Spot, and have a taste of the delicious dinner and the roaring Bonfire specially arranged for the OFT guests by the Base administration & in shaa Allah we return to Islamabad at around 9PM.

Trip Costs : 3000Rs per person

Means of Transport:

1. (Toyota Hiace High Roof Vans - 10 seater)

2. 4x4's (Toyota Vigo &/or Land cruisers)
